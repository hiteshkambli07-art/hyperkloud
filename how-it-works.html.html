How It Works

The Driftflow Engine.

Three phases. One team. Every tool working together. We turn buying signals into booked meetings — without you lifting a finger.

1

ICP & Market Intelligence

Mapping your market before we send a single email

  • Deep ICP research and buyer persona mapping
  • TAM definition across your target geographies
  • Signal detection: hiring, funding, tech stack, LinkedIn activity
  • Competitor and category positioning analysis
  • Scoring model calibrated to your specific product
  • Channel-market fit: email vs LinkedIn vs both
2

Multi-Channel System Design

Architecting the full outbound and RevOps stack

  • Cold email infrastructure: domains, warmup, Zapmail deliverability
  • LinkedIn outreach sequences via HeyReach
  • Claude-powered personalisation at scale — every message unique
  • HubSpot CRM architecture and pipeline governance
  • n8n workflows connecting every tool end-to-end
  • Warm lead triggers: opens, clicks, replies forwarded to you

Execution, Optimisation & Scale

Running and iterating what actually works

  • Multi-channel campaign execution — 7 touches over 14 days
  • Response management and lead qualification
  • Weekly performance analysis and attribution
  • Continuous optimisation: double down on winners
  • Executive dashboards: pipeline, forecast, funnel metrics
  • Monthly strategy review — real RevOps thinking, not reporting
