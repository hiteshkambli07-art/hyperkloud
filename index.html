Frequently Asked Questions
How does Driftflow measure success?
We measure success by the number of warm leads delivered, meetings booked, and pipeline generated. You'll see a clear weekly report showing exactly what was found, emailed, warmed up, and handed over — with scores for every lead.
Can't we just hire more SDRs?
You could. A junior SDR costs €40,000-60,000/year and spends 80% of their time on research — not calls. Driftflow does all that research automatically for a fraction of the cost, so your existing SDRs can focus entirely on closing.
I'm unsure where to start with AI SDRs.
Start with a free trial. We'll find 20 warm leads for your business in the first week at no cost. You'll see exactly how it works before committing to anything.
What is outbound orchestration?
Outbound orchestration is connecting signals, data, channels and tasks into one flow. A good signal becomes a scored account, then a clear SDR task with enough context to act — all automatically, without manual work.
What if leads don't reply?
We only hand over leads who have already shown interest — opened emails, clicked links, or triggered buying signals. Your SDR never calls cold. If a batch underperforms, we top it up with additional leads at no extra cost.
